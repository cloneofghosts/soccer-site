About the AISL

The AISL was established in 2009 to provide a local and affordable option for residents to play indoor soccer during the winter months. The current day-to-day operations of the league are managed by sibblings Kevin Read and Andrea Edmonds-Read, who took over in 2011.

The AISL has grown to now consist of four ever-growing divisions, where some divisions have competitive and recreational tiers. The AISL runs over the course of 2x15-week sessions, Fall (Sept - Jan) and Spring (Jan - May), and averages 32 teams per session.

The AISL would not exist without the support of the Beckwith Township Recreation Department, the Carleton Place Soccer Club, the AISL referee panel, team managers, and all of our players.

We do this for the love of the game. We hope you have an enjoyable and safe experience playing in the AISL.
