About The AISL

The AISL indoor season is comprised of competitive and recreational teams in four divisions competing across two or three sessions.

All games held at the Beckwith Recreation Complex in Beckwith, Ontario.

The league is an Ontario Soccer sanctionned league through the Carleton Place Soccer Club.

Sessions

Fall Session: September - January (15 games)

Spring Session: January - May (15 games)
